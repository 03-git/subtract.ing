# Pattern

Anyone with a domain can make their web presence agent-first.

## Structure

yourdomain.com/
├── index.html     # Trust signal. "Ask your AI about this."
├── llms.txt       # Manifest. Everything an agent needs.
└── [topic].txt    # Depth as needed.

## Requirements

- Domain: $12/year
- Hosting: Free (GitHub Pages, Cloudflare Pages)
- Content: .txt files

No CMS. No framework. No build step.

## Why

Humans don't read websites. They ask AI to read for them.
Your llms.txt is what the AI actually sees.
Your index.html is proof you're real.
