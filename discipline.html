The Loop

Before the agent acts
  1. Verify last signature
  2. Surface unsigned drift
  3. Human decides: sign, continue, or abort
After the session
  1. Update manifest
  2. Sign

The agent never acts on unsigned state without surfacing it.
The human never leaves without signing what happened.

This is the loop. It runs every session.

The architecture you read in /governance implements this loop.
The primitives you read in /substrate make it possible.
The philosophy you read in /why explains why it matters.
